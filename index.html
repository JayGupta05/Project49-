    Interesting facts about Origami:-
  1. Origami was derived from 2 Japanese Words- Ori(folded) Kami(paper).
  2. Before that, the art was called "Orikata".
  3. An ancient Japanese legend says that if you fold one thousand cranes you will be granted a wish.

    Facts about a squirrel:-
  • Squirrels are members of the family Sciuridae, a family that includes small or medium-size rodents.
  • The squirrel family includes tree squirrels, ground squirrels, chipmunks, marmots, flying squirrels, and prairie dogs amongst other rodents.
